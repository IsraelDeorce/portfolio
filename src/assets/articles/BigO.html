Time Complexity

Is the language and metric we use to describe the efficiency of algorithms.

O (Time Complexity): Upper bound (worse case).

Ω (Time Complexitymega): Lower bound (best case).

Θ (big theta): Tight bounds (expected case).

From best to worse:

  1. Θ(1) - Constant
  2. Θ(log n) - Logarithmic
  3. Θ(n) - Linear
  4. Θ(n log n) - Constant
  5. Θ(n²)
  6. Θ(nˣ)
  7. O(n!)
